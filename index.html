About

Brief

Hi I am Abhishek Sharma currently working at Vedanta Resources. I have done my Bachelors in Technology from Indian Institute of Technology Kanpur (IITK). I aim to pursue a career in data science and applied research along with exploring a plethora of applications cutting across business analytics, sustainability and healthcare. During my undergraduate years, I have pursued various advanced courses in Engineering and Finance, such as Financial Econometrics, Hydrodynamic Stability, Transport Phenomena and BioElectricity. I have explored my interest in Data Science through various corporate research projects and academic endeavors developing a keen interest in the field of data science.
