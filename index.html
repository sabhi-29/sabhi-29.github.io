Hi I am Abhishek Sharma currently pursuing my Bachelors in Technology (B.Tech.) from Indian Institute of Technology Kanpur. Being in my final semester now I could say that I have had an exquisitive journey filled with many thrills, joys and pot holes as well of course :P And no, specializing in chemical engineering does not mean I like chemistry! Infact I hate it as much as you do. In addition to that I like music, watching movies, clicking photos. Cliché right? Well..... actutally not. You see in each of the three hobbies I mentioned I have tried my hand and as a matter of fact pursue them more than my studies (I shouldn't have said this but who cares ¯\_(ツ)_/¯ ). And yes, you can also associate any stigma you have about a boy who is from Devbhoomi (Uttrakhand for nutheads) and lives in Kanpur. (although not now, but you get the point) Welcome to my homepage!
